Pac-man Post Self-Defense Workshop (working title)

  • High Concept: RPG fight pac-man
  • Player Experience Goals: Play experience of Pac Man but with options to fight ghosts rather than immediate game over, risk-reward.
  • Genre: Arcade Game w/ RPG stuff
  • Game play: Plays like pac-man with the exception that you stop and fight ghosts rather than simply lose.
  • Prototype goal: Prototype should evaluate whether the addition of fighting to pac-man is interesting.
  • Features: Fast arcadey fun with additional element of risk and reward.
  • Setting/Premise: The game is set in a labyrinth and you are a knight fighting monsters
  • Story: You bring your own interpretation
  • Target Audience: People who enjoy classic arcade games but would like to see more modern gameply additions that remove some of the quarter-hungryness.
  • Play time: target of 5 to 30 minutes depending on how well you do, prototype will be much shorter.
  • Strategies: Avoid conflict where possible but take advantage of more winnable fights to get out of difficult situations.
  • Prototype Assets: Richard Davey - Phaser Coding Tips, How to Move Like PacMan
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: pac-man!
